bluffcon's happy bee

similar: Rickroll Wikipedia

The Bluffcon's Happy Bee (different from a regular happy bee) is one of bluffcon's many creations. It is a rounded insect with 6 legs, 2 large wings, a big stinger, and a happy smile. It is colloquially believed that the smile was the reason for the naming of the Happy Bee.

While not a real animal, it was made as a placeholder for the wiki page template's info table's image slot. There is an interesting legend following this bee, which is covered in the History section.

Speculation

While it is theoretically possible that this bee will eventually become real, a number of precautions must be met to make sure the bee will behave properly. Many speculate that this bee will never even become an actual article in the wiki and will only stay a template page. These speculations, while reasonable, should be discussed only within a reasonable distance from the Happy Bee. It will sting you to death.
